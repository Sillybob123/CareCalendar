Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 16, 2025

The Catalyst Magazine ("we," "us," or "our") operates the CareCalendar mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy is intended to inform you about how we handle information in the App.

Your privacy and the security of your health information are our top priorities. CareCalendar is designed to be a private, secure space for you to manage your health.

Information We Collect

To provide personalized health recommendations, we collect the following information:

  • Account Information: When you sign up, we collect your name and email address to create and manage your account. This is handled securely through Firebase Authentication.
  • Health Profile Information: You provide personal health data, including but not limited to, your date of birth, biological sex, medical history, family history, and lifestyle factors (e.g., smoking status). This information is essential for our screening engine to generate accurate recommendations.
  • Medication Information: Any prescription details you add, such as medication name, dosage, and schedule, are stored to provide reminders and tracking.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to operate and improve the App's functionality. Specifically:

  • To generate personalized health screening and wellness recommendations.
  • To provide medication and appointment reminders.
  • To display your health data and trends within the App.
  • To create your personal health calendar.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal health information with any third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

Third-Party Services

CareCalendar uses trusted third-party services to function:

  • Firebase (by Google): We use Firebase for secure user authentication and to store your encrypted health profile data.
  • Apple HealthKit: If you grant permission, the App will access data from the Health app on your device to automatically populate your health metrics. This data is processed on your device and is subject to Apple's HealthKit privacy policies.

Data Storage and Security

Your health data is stored securely using Firebase's encrypted databases. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. Data stored locally on your device (e.g., saved prescriptions) is protected by your device's native operating system security.

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at stemcatalystmagazine@gmail.com.
