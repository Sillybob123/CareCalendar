Important Medical Disclaimer

CareCalendar is an informational tool and self-management companion. It is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. The screening recommendations provided are based on general guidelines (such as USPSTF and CDC) and may not apply to every individual situation. Always consult with a qualified healthcare provider for any health concerns or before making any medical decisions. If you think you are experiencing a medical emergency, call your local emergency number immediately.
